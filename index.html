About Block Blast

Block Blast is an appealing puzzle game where you need to move blocks, like the traditional Tetris game. Thanks to this game, you can relax and train your brain for free on the web or on your phone at any time. 

Your main objective is to clear the rows and columns to get the best score. When you can't put down any more blocks, the game is over. 

How to play Block Blast 

  • To play the Block Blast game, you need to open your browser and access our website: https://blockblastgame.gitlab.io/
  • Start the game: To play the Block Blast game, click the "OK, Play Now" button on the screen.
  • Drag and drop blocks into the grid to fill rows and columns.
  • Complete a row or column to earn points.
  • Place bricks until they no longer fit on the grid.
  • Don't place blocks randomly; instead, you need to have a movement strategy to maximize your score.

Tips and Tricks to get the highest score

You may prolong your Block Blast gameplay and achieve remarkable scores by using these pro tips:

  • Don't place blocks haphazardly: Take a moment to look at the board and speculate on the possible locations of the next blocks.
  • Keep space for Large Blocks: If you haven't made space for large blocks, the game will be over. So, you must keep areas that are adaptable open, especially along the center and at corners.
  • Build for Combos: While it's acceptable to clear a single line, the best points and eye-catching images occur when you clear two or more lines simultaneously, or a combo. Make sure your placements set up possible multi-line clears, because occasionally giving up a short point now can result in a huge combo later on.
  • Keep the board balanced by avoiding piling all of your blocks on one side. When challenging shapes come up, keep everything level and open to give you more room to work with.
